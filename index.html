Our Products

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk foam.

Latte

A latte or caffè latte is a milk coffee that is a made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top

Espresso

Espresso is a coffee-brewing method in which a small amount of nearly boiling water is forced under pressure through finely ground coffee beans.

Flat White

A flat white is a coffee drink consisting of espresso and steamed milk. It generally has a higher proportion of espresso to milk than a caffè latte, and lacks the thick layer of foam in a cappuccino.

Black Coffee

Black Coffee is Coffee made by adding hot water to ground Coffee without milk or cream finely. It has a super strong flavour of Coffee.

Irish Coffee

Irish coffee is a different type of coffee that includes alcohol and is popular during the Christmas period.
